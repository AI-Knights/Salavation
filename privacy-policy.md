# Salvation

Welcome to the official documentation for the Salvation mobile application.

Salvation is a secure employee management platform developed for Master Tattoo Institute to simplify workforce management, attendance tracking, task assignment, and employee accountability.

- Photo Verification

- Real-Time Reporting
